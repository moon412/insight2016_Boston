About Home Health Helper

Home Health Helper aims to reduce the number of home health agencies with unsatisfied readmission rate by improving the service quality.

The Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) provides the Provider of Services (POS) files containing the characteristics of home health facilities. Medicare provides the quality measures of home helath agencies' services and readmission rates. The United States Census Bureau's API provides census data on block, tract, county and state levels.

Based on machine learning models, the bar graph on the left shows the importance of these aspects in reducing the readmission.

What is CCN?

CCN stands for the CMS Certification Number.

Each home health agency has a unique CCN.

For example, 17069 is Amedisys home health of Foley.
